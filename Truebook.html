Mobile Application Objectives

The general purpose of the study is to enhance the reading comprehension of the Grade 11 senior high school students whoâ€™s using the mobile application as a supplementary learning material.

  1. To develop a mobile learning application for students to analyze the context of the story with the use of vocabulary, monitor their reading comprehension progress per level, and identify their level of reading comprehension.
  2. To implement In-place Algorithm in a mobile application to help grade 11 senior high school students in improving their reading comprehension regarding doing the assessment tasks after reading.
  3. To evaluate the application using ISO 9126 as an evaluation tool for measuring the quality standard using criteria: Functionality, Usability, Efficiency.

Application Features

  • Skills Exam- an examination for the Grade 11 senior high students, to determine their baseline knowledge and categorize their level of reading comprehension skill with the use of the following simulations drag and drop, identification, and, multiple choice approach.

  • Reading Comprehension test- a test given after the student's completion of reading the stories provided and measure their achievement and effectiveness based on the situation/questions presented.

  • Progress Monitoring- this module identifies the stages the achievements of the students based on the level of reading comprehension position.

  • Vocabulary- this module contains words that will serve as hints to guide the grade 11 Senior High School students to understand the stories comprehensively.

  • Simulations-a drag and drop and multiple-choice type of visual graphics assessment tasks.

Delimitations of the Mobile Application

  1. This mobile learning application is available on Android devices with version 4.1 (Jelly Bean) and above.

  2. This mobile learning will only cover the learning stories approved by the grade 11 Professor.

  3. The application does not serve as replacement medium of enhancing the reading skills of the students in improving their reading comprehension but will only serve as a supplementary tool for their activities and learning.
